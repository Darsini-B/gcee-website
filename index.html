About Government College of Engineering, Erode

Welcome to Government College of Engineering, Erode

Government College of Engineering, Erode (formerly Institute of Road and Transport Technology – IRTT) is one of the premier government engineering institutions in Tamil Nadu. Established in 1984 under the Institute of Road and Transport by the Tamil Nadu State Transport Corporation, the college was created to provide quality technical education to students across the state. In 2021, the institution was transferred to the Directorate of Technical Education (DoTE), Government of Tamil Nadu and renamed as Government College of Engineering, Erode. The college is affiliated to Anna University, Chennai and approved by AICTE, New Delhi. Located in a green campus between Chithode and Bhavani, the institution offers excellent infrastructure, modern laboratories, library facilities, and experienced faculty members. GCEE provides undergraduate and postgraduate engineering programs and encourages students to participate in research, innovation, and industry collaborations. With strong academic standards and an active placement cell, the college continues to produce skilled engineers who contribute to technological and societal development.
